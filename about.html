How it started

From Idea
to Reality

H&S Online Store was founded by two friends — Hamza Saleem and Shuja ul Hassan — with one shared belief: great products should not come with a premium price tag.

Starting with a small catalog of hand-picked tech accessories and fashion items, we grew by listening to our customers and relentlessly curating quality. Every product in our store is selected with care — because we shop here too.

Today, H&S serves customers across Pakistan with fast delivery, transparent pricing, and a promise that stands behind every order.
